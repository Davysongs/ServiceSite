F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer a warranty on your refurbished phones and accessories?

    Yes, we provide a warranty on all our refurbished products. Our warranty ensures that you receive high-quality devices and accessories that are backed by our commitment to customer satisfaction.

  • What types of training programs do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of training programs, from basic electronics troubleshooting to advanced mobile and PC repair techniques. Our courses cater to beginners and those looking to enhance their technical skills in the electronics field.

  • How long does it typically take to repair a mobile phone or PC?

    The repair time can vary depending on the complexity of the issue. Our team strives to provide efficient service, and many common repairs can be completed within the same day. For more detailed estimates, feel free to contact us.

  • Can I purchase SIM cards for international use from your store?

    Yes, we offer SIM cards suitable for international travel. These SIM cards provide cost-effective options for staying connected while abroad, with various data and call plans to choose from.

  • Are your trainers experienced professionals?

    Absolutely. Our training programs are led by experienced professionals with in-depth knowledge of electronics and repair techniques. You can trust that you'll receive top-quality instruction and guidance.

  • How can I get in touch with your customer support team?

    You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels. Feel free to visit our contact page on our website, call our dedicated support line, or send us an email. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
